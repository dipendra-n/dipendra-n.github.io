Techniche Android Application

UI/UX design

  1. Portfolio
  2. Techniche Android Application

Brief
Techniche is the annual techno-management festival of Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati. The project was aimed to create an android app which would provide the relevant information related to the festival. The app would be used by the participants/potential participants of Techniche. I was responsible for the design of the app, while Mr. Rahul Muttineni developed it. I conducted 5 open ended semi structured interviews with the stakeholders (3 fest organizers) and potential participants (2 IITG and 2 non-IITG students). After identifying the needs from the interviews, I worked on the information architecture, followed by low fidelity wire framing and the UI mockups. The app is live on the google play store and has about a 1000 downloads.
