Tangify: An Interactive Learning Toolkit

Tangible User Interfaces

  1. Portfolio
  2. Tangify

Tangify is Tangible Learning Toolkit that has been specially designed for specially abled children of age group of 3-7 years. The targeted solution mainly targets three disabilities including autism, cerebral palsy and ADHD. In the current system that we have designed, children can learn about different characteristics of a fruits by placing different tokens of the fruit in different compartments of the Tangify platform.
